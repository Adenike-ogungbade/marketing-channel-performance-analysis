## Funnel Analysis

Funnel analysis was conducted to evaluate user progression from impressions to clicks and final activation across marketing channels.

### Key Observations

- Referral channels demonstrate strong conversion efficiency across all funnel stages, indicating high user intent and trust.
- Google Ads shows solid click-through rates but moderate drop-off at the activation stage.
- Paid Social generates high impressions but experiences significant drop-off after the click stage, suggesting lower user intent or ineffective targeting.

### Business Implications

- Paid Social campaigns may need improved audience targeting and landing page optimization to increase conversions.
- Google Ads campaigns can be optimized to improve post-click engagement.
- Referral channels should be further scaled due to their strong end-to-end funnel performance.
